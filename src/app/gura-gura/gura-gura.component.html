Gura Gura No Mi

The Gura Gura no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit which allows the user to create vibrations, or "quakes", making the user a Tremor Human (震動人間, Shindō Ningen?). It was eaten by Edward Newgate, but upon his death, its power was stolen by Marshall D. Teach. This fruit is fearsomely reputed to be able to destroy the world, and is considered to be the strongest Devil Fruit within the Paramecia class, having powers no weaker than those of a Logia Devil Fruit.
