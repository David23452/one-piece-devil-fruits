Bomu Bomu No Mi

The Bomu Bomu no Mi is a Paramecia-type Devil Fruit that allows the user to make any part of their body explode, whether it be their limbs, hair, mucus, even breath or cough, making the user a Bomb Human (爆弾人間, Bakudan Ningen?, VIZ: "Human Dynamite"). It was eaten by Gem, better known by his Baroque Works alias Mr. 5.
